Our Story

The House of Humanity was founded with a simple yet powerful belief: that every person deserves dignity, opportunity, and hope. What started as a grassroots effort to help families in crisis has grown into a comprehensive organization dedicated to creating lasting change.

Our journey began when founder Bridget Charles witnessed firsthand the challenges facing vulnerable communities. Driven by compassion and a determination to make a difference, she established The House of Humanity to be more than just a helping hand – to be a bridge to self-sufficiency and empowerment.

Today, we continue to expand our reach and impact, always staying true to our core mission of making a way for those who need it most.
