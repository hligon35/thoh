Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I request assistance?

You can call us, visit our office, or fill out the contact form above. We'll schedule an intake appointment to assess your needs.

What documents do I need to bring?

Bring a valid ID, proof of income, and any relevant documentation related to your situation. Our staff will guide you through the process.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! Contact us to learn about current opportunities, complete our volunteer application, and attend orientation.

Are donations tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible and you'll receive a receipt for your records.

Do you provide services outside your location?

We primarily serve our local community, but some programs may extend to neighboring areas. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

What should I do in an emergency?

For immediate emergencies, call 911. For urgent assistance related to housing, food, or safety, call our 24/7 crisis line at (555) 987-6543.
