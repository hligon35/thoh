Meet the Founder

The retired veteran with an unshakeable passion to serve humanity

Bridget Charles

Retired Veteran & Founder

Bridget Charles is a retired veteran who founded The House of Humanity with an unrelenting devotion to serve humanity. Inspired by her deep sense of duty cultivated during military service, she established our organization as a beacon of hope for families struggling with homelessness and domestic violence.

Even after serving her country, Bridget stays motivated by her commitment to giving back to the community. Her vision for The House of Humanity is to create a haven where homeless families and victims of domestic violence can find the support they need to pursue their best possible well-being in a safe and secure environment.

"We strive to be a cornerstone in the lives of homeless families and domestic violence victims, providing them with the support needed to overcome adversity. Our dedication extends beyond providing refuge to empowering individuals to rewrite their narratives, embrace their potential, and create a hopeful future."
Bridget Charles Retired Veteran & Founder, The House of Humanity

Philosophy & Approach

Bridget's approach to helping families in crisis is rooted in her military training and deep understanding of the importance of secure, supportive environments. She believes in addressing not just immediate needs, but providing comprehensive support that leads to long-term stability and self-sufficiency.
