About Community Hearts

Serving our community with dignity and compassion since 2010

Our Mission

Community Hearts Food Pantry is dedicated to alleviating hunger and food insecurity in our local community. We believe that access to nutritious food is a basic human right.

Our Vision

A community where everyone has access to nutritious food and the support they need to thrive. We envision a world without hunger.

Our Values

Compassion, dignity, respect, and community. We treat every person with kindness and work together to create positive change.

Our Services

Comprehensive support for families facing food insecurity
